How We Work

After an initial meeting we compile and send over a complete proposal with associated costs and guideline printing and material costs depending on your requirements.

We create as many concept routes, style boards and source any other inspiration we feel is appropriate.

After developing up the chosen concept we supply proofs and samples if required and organise local printing or can supply print ready files if preferred.

We work closely with our couples to make sure every detail is right - even down to sticking stickers and tracking down matching stamps!

Everything is delivered on a next day signed for recorded delivery when complete, or delivered by hand depending on location and availability.

Specialist Requirements

Br&newweddings have a vast network of craftsmen, printers and specialists who they can call upon for various requests including ceramics, woodwork, illustration, printing, letterpress, embossing and de-bossing, foiling and finishing.

These extra processes can add so much depth to a design and are discussed and costed per project accordingly.

A Br&new story

Having worked for award winning graphic and product design consultancies in the big smoke for 8 years our designer Freya decided it was time for a change and set about starting Br&newweddings and our parent company TheBr&newstudio LTD in 2012.

Whilst TheBr&newstudio concentrates on the more corporate world of graphics designing brands, logos, websites and corporate identities for some fantastic clients, Br&newweddings sets about making your wedding days beautiful and absolutely unique using all our skills, experience and inspiration.

About me!

Hello, I'm Freya founder and designer at Br&newweddings, lover of leopard print, fizz & dinner parties! Having worked in the wedding industry for over 5 years we were excited to plan our own big day last November (you can see our stationery over in the portfolio section).

In my home studio I spend my days creating pretty things and am passionate that your wedding day should reflect you both as a couple which is why each and every wedding we create at Br&new is completely unique or totally "brand new" (see what we did there!).

I am a total stationery geek and am always on the lookout for new ways of creating fabulous paper goods & making them that bit more unique from other off the shelf designs.

If I'm not working you can find me in the garden or tearing up the hills on my mountain bike.. anything outdoors is my timeout.

I can't wait to hear from you and find out about your own wedding plans!
