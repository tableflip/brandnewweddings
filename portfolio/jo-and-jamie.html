Jo and Jamie

Woodland Wonderland

From our first meeting with Jo & Jamie we knew we would love the result of their stationery. There was chat of tractor rides, woodland animals, hay bales, and an OWL delivering the ring down the aisle! AMAZING!

The result was this spectacular illustration depicting all the animals which could be found in the woodland around the reception venue which was atop a hill at Jo's family home. Jo & Jamie wanted a rustic theme but also something unique and the personal detail in the invitations really delivered this.

Instead of a standard invitation suite we created a little booklet which held all the information required for quests, along with a little RSVP postcard.

Their table names were each one of the animals from the invitations and it was all finished off with the most beautiful map showing the venues and travel info.

Enjoy!

We loved everything
