Lorna and Nathan

Geometrics at Babington

Over a few coffee's at a fab coffee shop in Little Venice we got to hear about Lorna & Nathan's fantastic weekend adventure they were taking their guests on.

Scandi, modern, geometric and metallic were the descriptive words flying around and we were so excited to be working on such a different style of stationery.

Their invitations were to come as part of specially compiled goodie boxes including a whole weekend set of treats from toothbrushes to shampoo, alka skeltza and sweets! All with their own branded cotton bag and their invitations sat on top perfectly.

The style of the day worked perfectly through the stationery suite from quirky on the wonk escort cards to their pick and mix table cards (yes guests picked which table to sit at from a lucky dip.... LOVE!)

Table names were places which meant something special to Lorna & Nathan and the order of service started with a fabulous "nice to meet you" section describing the wedding party.

Certainly one of our favourite designs so far!

The stationery looked fabulous and really added to the look and feel of the wedding, stunning job. Thank you.

Mr & Mrs Oakley
