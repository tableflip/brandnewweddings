Stephanie & Adam

Irish Landscape

We loved chatting through Stephanie & Adam's ideas over a coffee at their home in Surrey. All the wedding idea's were strewn across the table and I could see the excitement in their eyes as they chatted about their plans. Their wedding was back in a tiny corner of Ireland where Stephanie had grown up. The local church for the venue and a marquee in her parents paddock for the reception. Beer barrel tables, wind turbines, the family horses and the two gorgeous dogs all featured in these wonderful landscape concertina invitations depicting the venues and location of the day.

The hand drawn illustration with little caricatures of the bride and groom really helped set the day with a rustic but personal feel. The Irish font kept the styling on-point and the babies breath flower details matched Stephanie's bouquet on the day.

We also created beautiful Order of Services for their ceremony featuring the church and caricatures, and little details throughout keeping the day seamless.

From our initial meeting with Freya it was clear to see the talent she has in understanding the look and feel you are going for and being able to translate this into visually stunning wedding stationery. It was amazing to see all the ideas we discussed come together and we could not have been happier with the results. The feedback we have gotten from family and friends has been incredible - best invitations they have ever seen! I cannot recommend Freya enough.

Stephanie & Adam
