Ashleigh & Jamie

Vintage Village Hall

We were so lucky to be asked by Ashleigh & Jamie to create their wedding stationery. Their plans for a vintage, fun filled, May-Day wedding down in sunny West Sussex sounded amazing, and the attention to detail was beyond comprehension!

We kicked the day off with a bespoke may-day poem for their little fold out save the date's along with limited edition penny black stamps, and followed it up with a beautiful kraft and floral ribbon invitations complete with a black and white postcard RSVP showing their ceremony venue and "travel in style" inserts.

On the day I must have spent a good few hours looking at everything they had created, and I'm still not sure if I saw it all - from the most insane photo booth 'living room' to a vintage typewriter that guests left messages on, to a box of vintage games, to gorgeous birdcage centrepieces the venue was transformed from a quaint village hall to a display of unique and personal details which would put any wedding planner to shame! Well done guys! It was a pleasure to have shared your day with you.

It was an absolute pleasure working with Freya, her energy and enthusiasm is infectious and her ability to put together ideas really made the products come alive. We gave her some ideas and themes but to be honest we were clueless on what we really wanted. The ideas and samples we got back though were truly amazing and it wasn't long before we were sending them out in the post. If you are looking for professionally crafted stationery with a personality and flair, all bespoke and made to your taste, please get in touch with Freya. It will be the best decision you can make.

Jamie & Ashleigh Stevenson
