Georgina & David

Tropical Fun

We were so lucky to start working with Georgina & David nearly 2 years before their wedding day! It sounds like an age but it meant we could totally get involved in the whole day and make sure the stationery was consistent and held the whole thing together from the save the dates all the way through to thank you cards. The brief was tropical, fun, slightly rustic and colourful so alot of research was needed to get the designs spot on!

The whole suite was matched to the turquoise Pantone wedding & Dessy bridesmaids dress swatch and we managed to match ribbon, graphics and designs to these perfectly. They featured little caricatures of the bride and groom and hand illustrated flowers which reflected the bouquets and displays on the day itself.

There were so many highlights, Frankfurter their GORGEOUS sausage dog being the ring bearer in his bespoke bowtie and matching lead, the sausage dog Grey Goose vodka luge, the amazing flowers which were designed to match our illustrations perfectly, the bouncy castle... where do we stop!

One of our favourite parts was seeing all their photos popping up online and nearly ALL of them featuring our stationery. The attention to detail had not gone unnoticed. The Order of Service Fan was a firm favourite with bespoke caricatures of the entire bridal party on one side and the service on the other.. along with the details throughout the evening, little messages on toilet luxuries, flipflop baskets, smores jars... everything had been thought about and it all looked stunning on the day.

Huge congratulations to Mr & Mrs Stebbings, we wish you the very best in your married life together.

I can't stress enough how amazing Freya has been.. even when I was at my most bridezilla Freya was there with her calming emails and level head and got everything done perfectly in time. The stationery was amazing, to such a high standard and Freya was awesome every step of the way... from mixing together a number of ideas to finding the EXACT colour ribbon and matching everything that I wanted. Cannot recommend her enough... use Br&newweddings!! xx

Mrs Stebbings
