Karen & Ben

Black Tie Sophistication

Karen and Ben came to us with a clear idea of a design they wanted, they had struggled to find someone who could make their vision reality and a printers could not offer the right level of design input for them. Br&newweddings were able to help by detailing and developing their idea into a polished and beautiful design incorporating all the elements that the couple wanted.

The illustrative black and white design was used to commemorate Karen's late Grandmother 'Ivy,' and the monogram of the couples initials brought the stationery together throughout the lead-up and on the day itself.

We helped tie everything together from the pocketfold invitations, to the thank you cards and everything in between. The stationery reflected the elegant and sophisticated day Karen and Ben had planned which included top hats, wine pairing and fantastic food with the gorgeous backdrop of Clearwell Castle.

We think the final effect of the black and cream design was so striking and fitted their smart and sophisticated day perfectly.
