Emma & Jeff

Florals at Rudding Park

We always love it when a wedding & events planner get's in touch to design their wedding stationery, and this one is a stunner.
Emma, daughter of England cricket legend Geoffrey Boycott, and her fiancé Jeff had strong ideas for their stationery. Bold watercolours and deep grey stationery enhanced with gorgeous ivory with gold foiling set the scene for their big day at the incredible Rudding Park. What a colour combo!
Starting out with their gorgeous 'pencil us in' save the dates with bespoke engraved pencils and luxury tags, we followed with the most striking invitations, wrapped in soft touch and individually named ivory belly bands, concertina rsvp's with gorgeous floral details, menu choices for each guest to reply easily and finished with an insert card holding all the important details - the suite looked divine.
For the on the day pieces we created a stunning hanging table plan, each name with its own individual disk fixed under its table name with gold links and finished with matching satin ribbon its certainly an instant favourite. Their order of service used our popular vertical half fold style which sits perfectly on a church pew and looks oh so elegant, again donning the gorgeous hot pink and lilac watercolour florals and deep grey background. Other details included on the day signage for restrooms, dancing shoes and framed order of the day which helped guests know what was in store for them over the duration of the celebrations!
What a joy!

I am so pleased we used Freya for our wedding stationery. I originally got in touch with her after seeing one of her previous designs but we ended up with a bespoke stationery suite with completely different ideas and we couldn’t have been happier. From our ‘pencil us in’ save the dates to our life sized cherry blossom tree table plan, I wanted every part to be unique and a bit different. Freya was fantastic at making amends and suggestions to our ideas and helped source every aspect of our designs which was a huge help. She’s always quick to reply and turn things around, even when getting married herself and has been professional throughout. Thank you Freya!

Emma & Jeff Kendrick
