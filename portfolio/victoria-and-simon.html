Victoria & Simon

Micocoulier leaves & Rose gold foiling

I met Vicky & Si one weekend when they flew in from Geneva to see our beautiful samples. Having fallen in love with a design we had created a year previously there was little more to do than get in touch with Charlotte & Gerard and ask whether they would mind some of their details being used again. Being such a generous bride of course Charlotte didn't mind and we set about creating a bespoke design for V&S using a wallpaper'esq style pattern based on the Micocoulier leaves that grow on the tree's lining the drive at Simon's parents home in France. The whole suite was printed on gorgeous soft feel paper, double bonded for a fabulous heavy quality and finished off with the most beautiful rose gold foiling. We used textures to add a luxurious feel with a translucent cover to the little info booklet and held together with a matching rose gold bulldog clip and a stunning botanical illustration for the envelope liners.
Their on the day stationery followed suit and really helped the elegant and romantic sage colour scheme run through the whole day.

Freya was absolutely fantastic from start to finish. From travelling to see us on a Sunday at my mum's for the initial concepts to the end beautiful results. Cannot recommend her enough. It was such a fun experience. Her portfolio of designs are stunning! THANK YOU!!!! xxx

Victoria & Si Fuller
