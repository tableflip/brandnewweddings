Ella & James

Sunflowers at Waddesdon Dairy

Ella & James's day was such a colourful and fun event. They had fallen in love with our gorgeous hand illustrated designs so we set about creating a bright and colourful stationery suite based on the chosen florals for their day. Sunflowers and berries mixed with the striking architectural curls of New Zealand ferns which picked out Ella's mothers ancestry. The result, along with the dramatic grey and yellow invitation card and bespoke travel maps, were stunning and resulted in a whole set of stationery following through their day from the table plan to place name tags tied around napkins. Their gift list was the fabulous SmileTrain charity and their just giving page has raised over £3000 for the cause. Well done guys and congratulations!

We finally opened the invitations last night - and they are perfect. We love them. Thank you!
