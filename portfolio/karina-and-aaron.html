Karina & Aaron

Wedkandi Ibiza Wedding

We first met a very flustered Karina at the National Wedding Show, a worried bride who had a very specific idea about what she and her fiancé wanted for their wedding 'closing party' in the UK. Within minutes of chatting to us she was grinning from ear to ear and promptly headed to the champagne bar to celebrate!

Karina & Aaron met in Ibiza and spent a lot of time hitting the nightlife in their younger years and wanted to represent this at their party back in the UK, following on from a destination ceremony in sunny Ibiza.

Their plan was to throw a 'Wedkandi' club night... taking inspiration from the club scene headline brand Hedkandi. We were so excited when we heard the plan as this was right up our street! Br&newweddings created personal caricatures in the iconic Hedkandi style and developed a club flyer invitation that would inspire guests and set the scene perfectly. Party On!!

I was so lucky to find Freya as I was getting myself into a bit of a panic as I could not find anyone to design the type of invitations I was looking for since it was something a bit different. As soon as we started discussing my ideas she instantly made me feel better, it was not something she had done before but was excited about trying something new and she certainly did not disappoint. Freya managed to create an invitation that was even more perfect than I could of ever hoped for. It has been an absolute pleasure to work with Freya and I would definitely recommend her. In fact I have already had people ask where I got my invites made and I have happily passed on her details.

Karina
