Katie & Scot

Blush & Roses at Great Fosters

Wow! Really we could just stop with that one word. What a stunning day Katie & Scot managed to create. From the invitation suite to the flowers, to THAT dress, to the Green & Blacks individually wrapped chocolate bar favours and matching postage stamps we had an absolute ball working with this happy couple.

We worked with Katie & Scot over the space of 8 months creating a complete set of stationery for their flower filled day at Great Fosters, Surrey. The photographic background eluded to the opulent and romantic vibe of the day itself and everything from rsvp's, to thank you cards, and everything in between followed suit.

Katie was a joy to work with and kept us up to date with the antics of the gorgeous "Jeff the Pug" (...yes he has his own Instagram account) as we shared development images with her along the way. Never have we been so excited to receive photo messages!

The day itself was perfectly arranged with individually named place setting cards with menu choices and an order of the speeches on the reverse, finished of with the mini chocolate bar favours sealed with bespoke stickers, and surrounded by the most gorgeous floral displays imaginable by Rebecca at Laura Kuy flowers.

We wish Katie & Scot the hugest happiness in their married life & will certainly be in touch for more Jeff the Pug updates!

As soon as I met Freya I knew she would be the one to design our wedding stationery. From start to finish Freya was not only professional and easy to work with but she was fun and full of ideas and helpful little tips. She even managed to find some limited edition stamps that colour matched our invitations perfectly and most of our guests commented on how professional that was! I felt we bounced our ideas off each other and the stationery came out better than I could have imagined. Freya spent hours unwrapping and re-wrapping our chocolate bar favours with a hand made sleeve and also designed our thank you cards which carry on the theme. I would not hesitate to recommend Freya to anyone looking for unique and bespoke stationery that will stand out and I wish Freya all the success in the future with this fantastic business.

Katie Emma Singer
