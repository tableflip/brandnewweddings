Jessica & Matthew

Boxed Invitations in Sicily

We fell in love with Jessica & Matthews ideas as soon as we spoke to them about their destination wedding in sunny Sicily. Their initial travel-ticket save the dates set the scene for their guests giving intrigue but still keeping a classic and stylish theme together.

The beautiful boxed invitations followed, wrapped with ribbon and delivered in traditional brown parcel paper they were simply stunning. Each box had a folded invitation, bespoke map, rsvp and matching addressed envelope all embellished with a beautiful bespoke watermark pattern based on traditional Sicilian ceramic patterns and incorporating the couple's initials. The whole invitation was wrapped in a printed translucent photograph of the venue (also depicted on the save the dates) and secured with a ribbon band and tag to create a gorgeous luxury feel.

Their table plan and table names were created using a map depicting various journeys the couple had made together over time, keeping the day completely personal and fun whilst retaining their simple, stylish feel.

Freya was fantastic. She took my ideas and made them a beautiful reality. She was so personal and rushed things through when I was in a tight spot. I've had so many comments about my wedding stationary. Even my venue was gobsmacked when they saw the table plan! Fantastic!

Jessica Robertson
