Katarina and David

Italian Romance

We created these beautiful concertina style invitations for Katrina and her footballer hubby David who tied the knot in sunny Ravello, Italy.

They use our unique caricature style and match colours and features from their day. The gorgeous clifftop town of Ravello is featured alongside the backdrop of the Amalfi coast and the picturesque outdoor pavilion in which they held their ceremony.

Each guests name is printed on its own circular tag for a gorgeous personal detail and tied with a pretty peach ribbon, bringing the whole invitation together beautifully. The information is finished off with a bespoke map of Ravello showing some key landmarks and other points of interest for the wedding guests to explore on their trip.

Beautiful bespoke service, lots of patience, so personable! Really thought out and delivered my vision with perfection and complete ease. Have recommended endlessly!
